Gray Wolf

Under certain conditions, wolves can hear as far as six miles away in the forest and ten miles on the open tundra.

  • Binomial Name: Canis lupus
  • Lifespan: 6-8 years
  • Primary Diet: Carnivore
  • Biogeoraphic Regions: Neartic; palearctic

The ancestor of the domestic dog, gray wolf range in color fromgrizzled gray or black to all-white. The alpha female and alpha male usually mate for life.They are common in Canada and Alaska.
